Program for Algorithmic and Combinatorial Thinking (PACT)

I am currently a Mentor for PACT. I learn about randomized algorithms including applications of the Lovász Local Lemma to various problems such as k-SAT Also, I mentor students learning the mathematical foundations of computer science, involving subject such as combinatorics and graph theory.

Future Problem Solving (FPS)

I am on the school's FPS team. I work as part of a team to evaluate scenarios of the future, identifying the underlying problem and developing an action plan to solve this problem with an evaluation of its feasibility. My team did State Bowl Presentation of Action Plan Award: 2nd Place Freshman and Sophomore Year; 3rd Place Junior Year and the State Bowl Booklet Award: 3rd Place Junior Year.


CMPS

I participate in CMPS (Community Problem Solving). In the community, we focus on food waste and our efforts include such events as Sustainability Week culminating in the Sustainability Fair with attendance of around four hundred students. We spread awareness not only to students but also to community members through Farmer’s Markets as well as the Innovation Fair. To reduce food waste in our school, we use the scraps from the cafeteria to maintain our vermicomposting system.

Unbounded Futures

Unbounded Futures is dedicated to providing girls from the ages of 8-16 with another choice, to open up their eyes to the possibilities that exist outside of marriage, and prepare them for their journeys by equipping them with basic knowledge useful in any field. I am the founder and director.

Green Team

As a member of the Green Team, I work with a group of administrators and students as well as Sustainable Jersey to obtain grants for the school by documenting green practices in order to make our school more environmentally friendly.

Tutoring

I tutor as a summer job, part of National Honors Society and I am a teacher assistant. I was a Teacher’s assistant for Precalculus Honors and Accelerated Outside of school, I tutored high school students in Math subject including Algebra 2. I also design worksheets in accordance with the curriculum to help students learn.
